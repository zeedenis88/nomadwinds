Kite & work
a 4-week slow travel adventure for kitesurfing digital nomads.
The trip has been carefully designed for digital nomads and remote workers.
It’s a perfect balance between communal activities and personal time, allowing for a slow-paced exploration of Brazil’s breathtaking coastline—home to some of the best kitesurfing conditions in the world.

With steady winds blowing from morning to sunset at 20–35 knots and warm waters above 26°C, the conditions are ideal for all levels. Along the way, we’ll experience a mix of open ocean and flat-water lagoons, remote hideaways, and vibrant social hubs. Over four weeks, we’ll stay in three different locations, explore many more, and conclude our adventure in the legendary town of Jericoacoara.

Nomad Winds is more than just another kitesurfing trip—it’s a lifestyle. It’s designed for digital nomads and remote workers who want to combine work, adventure, and community but welcomes also all the longterm travellers.

This is an opportunity to meet your tribe, push your limits, and create lifelong connections.
